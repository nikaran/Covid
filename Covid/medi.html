Medical Assistance & Medicine Delivery



Our Medical Contingency Team consists of Doctors, Pharmacist, Medical Practicing Students Volunteers. Portal has the option of Telephonic Consultation, Pregnancy Helpdesk, Medicine Delivery, Treatment. Patients can order their Medicine through forms the given below by filling their name, age, sex, problem, uploading prescription (if needed). Once after your submission, it will verified and home delivered by the volunteer. In case of Emergency or Treatment needed, they have to select treatment option, so a doctor will be assigned to treat them in your place.
